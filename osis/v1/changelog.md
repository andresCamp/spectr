# Changelog

## 2026-03-24 — Identity pivot + monetization locked

### What changed
- **Product identity:** Spectr is "the most beautiful way to read and edit markdown on macOS." Not a thinking layer, not a spec tool. Beauty is the differentiator.
- **Monetization locked:** Alcove-style. OSS on GitHub, $14.99 one-time on App Store with 48-hour trial, Gumroad direct sales. No subscription ever.
- **Vision rewritten** from scratch. Old vision ("ambient thinking layer for spec-driven developers") archived — that identity belongs to osis, a future product that will fork Spectr.
- **Product spec rewritten** to reflect new identity, current feature set, and business model.
- **Phase 1 specs archived** — completed work moved to `archive/`.

### Why
The original vision conflated two products. Spectr is the beautiful markdown surface. Osis (future) is the spec-driven thinking layer. Separating them clarifies what Spectr is and who it's for: anyone who reads markdown and wants it to look good.
